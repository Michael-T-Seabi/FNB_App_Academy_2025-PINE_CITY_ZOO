# FNB_App_Academy_2025-PINE_CITY_ZOO
Third project in the FNB App Academy.
This is a full out map of Pine City Zoo, with the weather forecast, places to eat or have a picknic, full map of where to find the animals also a detailed listing of which animal you may encounter during the visit. Giving details of each area,so visitors my have a memorable an fun experience during the visit.
